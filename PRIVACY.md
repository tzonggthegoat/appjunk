# Privacy Policy for DISCOVR

**Last updated:** November 2025  
**Status:** Development version

DISCOVR is currently in development. This privacy policy outlines our data practices and will be expanded before public release.

## What We Collect

- **Pinterest account information**: Profile data and board content when you connect your Pinterest account
- **Taste preferences**: Your interactions, swipes, and selections used to build your taste profile
- **Usage data**: How you interact with generated magazines and content

## Why We Collect It

- To build and refine your personalized taste profile
- To generate magazines tailored to your interests
- To improve the app experience

## How We Protect Your Data

- Data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security policies
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

DISCOVR uses the following services:
- Pinterest API (for authentication and board imports)
- Supabase (for data storage)
- AI services (for magazine generation)

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

*A comprehensive privacy policy will be published before DISCOVR's public release.*
